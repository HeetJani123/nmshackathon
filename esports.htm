Esports Tournament

Showcase your skills in a thrilling 1v1 FC24 Kick Off challenge!

1. Introduction

Join us for a competitive esports event where participants will battle it out in FC24 on the PS5. This tournament is designed to foster innovation, sharpen gaming skills, and promote sportsmanship. Whether you're an experienced player or an intermediate, this is your chance to prove your worth.

2. Structure

  • Track: FC24 Kick Off Mode (1V1)
  • Participants: Individual players (no teams)
  • Eligible Teams: All football clubs except Soccer Aid
  • Schedule: Event begins at 10 AM with registration, followed by matches, leading to a final at 12 PM.

3. Guidelines

  • Eligibility: Open to high school students in Grades 10-12, aged 15 and above.
  • In-Game Decisions: All decisions by the in-game referee are final and cannot be contested.
  • Pauses: Maximum of two pauses per half.
  • Equipment Liability: Any damage caused to equipment will be the responsibility of the participant.
  • Rematches: Allowed only under emergency circumstances.

4. Evaluation

Matches will follow a knockout format. Winning players will advance to the next round until the final match determines the champion.

5. Learning Resources

Participants should be familiar with the game mechanics of FC24, particularly on the PlayStation platform (PS4 or PS5).

6. Tournament Approach

  • Time Management: Ensure proper squad preparation before each match.
  • Teamwork: Since this is an individual event, no collaboration is allowed between players. Any form of match-fixing will result in immediate disqualification.

7. Awards

Prizes: The top three players will receive exciting prizes, to be announced soon.

8. Mentorship & Support

Dedicated mentors will be available throughout the event at help desks to provide guidance and assistance.

9. Key Dates

Registration Deadline: One week prior to the event. Ensure you sign up in time to secure your spot.

10. Code of Conduct

  • Etiquette: Maintain professionalism and respect your opponents at all times.
  • Penalties: Players found restarting matches or violating time restrictions without permission will be disqualified.

11. FAQs

Which mode are we playing?

The tournament will be played in Kick Off mode, not Ultimate Team. You may choose any team except Soccer Aid.

Is this an individual or team event?

This is an individual competition, though multiple players from the same school can participate.

Will devices be provided?

Only the consoles for the matches will be provided; participants must bring their own controllers.

Are restarts allowed?

No, restarts are not allowed under any circumstances unless there is a critical issue.

Is there a setup time before the match?

Yes, each player will have 1 minute to select their team and configure their squad before the match begins.
