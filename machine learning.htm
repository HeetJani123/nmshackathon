Machine Learning Grade 9 and 10

1. Introduction

Overview: This hackathon focuses on creating machine learning models capable of detecting hidden objects in images. Teams will gather images, train their models, and test them on a final image. The objective is to foster innovation and creativity while making machine learning accessible to high school students.

Teams: Each team consists of 4 members with roles like developer, data handler, researcher, and presenter. The event will enhance practical coding and teamwork skills.

2. Event Structure

Challenge Focus: Machine learning for object detection using images.
Event Timing:

  • 9:30 AM: Introduction & Briefing
  • 9:45 AM: Image Training Session Begins
  • 10:30 AM: Testing Session Begins

3. General Guidelines

Eligibility: Open to high school students in grade 9 and 10 with beginner to intermediate machine learning knowledge.

Rules & Regulations: Teams must submit original work, properly cite external resources, and document their process for evaluation.

4. Evaluation Criteria

Criteria Weightage
Model Accuracy 40%
Image Selection Quality 20%
Model Efficiency 15%
Presentation & Explanation 15%
Documentation 10%

5. Learning Resources

Basic Skills: Participants should have knowledge of basic coding, image handling, and model-building platforms.

6. Awards & Recognition

Prizes:

  • 1st place: Gadgets + Certificates
  • 2nd place: Gadgets + Certificates
  • 3rd place: Certificates

7. FAQs

You will be provided a platform for image uploading, model building, and results submission.

The submission process will be explained during the event briefing.
